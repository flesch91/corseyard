Über ELEKS

ELEKS is an innovative global provider of software engineering and quality assurance services dedicated to helping industry leaders and technology challengers convert creative ideas into tangible business value. ELEKS delivers unique and reliable solutions for unparalleled business growth that include Data Science, Mobility, Digital Production and Financial solutions. With an intelligent mix of deep software engineering expertise, domain knowledge and strong scientific and mathematical proficiency, we go beyond traditional problem solving to create additional market advantages for businesses worldwide.

Since 1991, our software solutions have contributed to the success of our customers – including Fortune 500 companies – and have become a part of international best practices. ELEKS is a strategic partner with the proven ability to address the customers’ business needs in the most efficient manner. Our delivery organization consists of approximately 1,000 professionals in Eastern Europe and is further strengthened by a local presence in the U.S., Europe and the UK. For more information, please visit: www.eleks.com
